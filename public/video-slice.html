Slice/Trim Video

Welcome to the Slice/Trim Video feature.

Using this tool, you can cut your video into multiple segments by specifying the start and end times for each slice. Enter the times in the format of seconds or minutes and seconds (e.g., 90 or 1:30 for one minute and thirty seconds).

Steps to Slice Your Video:

1. Upload Your Video: Start by uploading the video you wish to edit.

2. Enter Time Segments: Enter the start and end times for the segments.

3. Create Video: Once all desired slices are set, click the 'Create Video' button. The tool will process these segments and join them into a single video file, which you can then download.
